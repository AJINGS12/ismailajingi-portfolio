The Challenge

Solving Real Problems

Keke riders in Kano face daily challenges with price negotiation, long waiting times, and inefficient driver-passenger matching. I set out to create a seamless digital solution.

Design Process

From Research to Reality

01

User Research

Conducted surveys with 20+ Keke riders using Google Forms and street interviews with drivers to understand pain points, behaviors, and needs.

02

Driver Insights

Deep-dive interviews revealed fuel waste from passenger searching and the critical need for efficient trip matching algorithms.

03

Visual Design

Developed high-fidelity screens using Keke-inspired green and yellow, ensuring cultural relevance and intuitive usability for diverse users.
